Principal & General Counsel

Monica co-founded a startup in the AI and blockchain convergence.

As a blockchain enthusiast, she is positioned to help fashion and drive the future of blockchain and cryptocurrency into its foreseeable worldwide marketspace.

She is bilingual in English and Chinese and is learning Korean and French. Last but not least to say, Monica is decorated with the United States Presidential Medal of Excellence.
